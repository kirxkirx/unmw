Find fields covering a sky position


Coordinates (R.A. Dec., J2000):

The tool runs sky2xy on every reference image and lists the fields covering the requested position, with pixel coordinates, distance to the nearest image edge, and a small thumbnail showing the position so you can judge whether the target is near the centre of a field or close to its edge.

Accepted coordinate formats:
  • Sexagesimal with colons: 17:45:37.199 -28:56:10.22
  • Sexagesimal with spaces: 17 45 37.199 -28 56 10.22
  • Decimal degrees: 266.4050 -28.9362
The Dec. sign may be +, -, or omitted for positive Dec.
